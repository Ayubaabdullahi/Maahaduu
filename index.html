History of Sheikh Muhammad Bello Boyi

🎥 Honor Video

TARIHIN GWANI
👶 Bith introduction Ranar haifuwa ▼

Sheikh Muhammad Bello Boyi (Abu Fatima, Gwani) was born in Sokoto, Ubandoma area, near Kofar Atiku, in 1389H (1968AD). He is 56 years old.

📖 Qur'an & Early Education ▼

From a young age, he showed love for the Qur'an; he completed its reading at age 11. At age 24, he memorized the entire Qur'an with its interpretation (Tafseer). This became the foundation of his pursuit of knowledge and teaching throughout his life.

👨‍🏫 Teachers ▼

He started studying under his father Malam Boyi, then continued with other great scholars including:

  • Malam Ummaru Babba (Rijiyar Zaure)
  • Malam Hussaini (Kofar Atiku)
  • Malam Muhammad Gwandu
  • Malam Ibrahim Bakane
  • Malam Muhammadu Gigani
  • Sheikh Sulaiman Al-Misri
  • Sheikh Abdullahi Zawawey Al-Makki
  • Sheikh Muhammad Al-Misri
  • and others.
🏫 Teaching Centers ▼

Sheikh Bello Boyi established centers in Sokoto, Gusau, Birnin Kebbi, Gwandu, and Jega. In Sokoto, there are several: His home in Ubandoma (Markazul Qur’an wal Ulum), Hubbaren Shehu, Imam Shadibi Mosque (Nakasari), Sultan Muhammad Bello Mosque (Kanwuri), Malam Aliu Gidan Kanawa's house, Malam Bello Gyal Gyal's house, Imam Nafi’u's house, and others.

📚 Students & Memorizers ▼

At his Hubbaren Shehu center, over 500 students have memorized the Qur'an in various fields — children, youth, and elders. Currently, more than 150 are awaiting their completion ceremony (Walima).

🕌 Tafseer & Imamate ▼

Sheikh conducts Tafseer lessons every Sunday morning at his home in Ubandoma. He is also the Friday Imam at Shadibi Mosque (Nakasari).

🌟 Character & Recognition ▼

Sheikh is known for his wisdom, understanding, humility, and good dealings. His words and actions have influenced thousands in Nigeria and beyond.

🎓 Honors ▼

On Saturday, April 20, 2024, Usmanu Danfodiyo University, Sokoto awarded him an honorary Doctor of Letters (Honoris Causa) for his contributions to religion, education, and humanity.
