Hifz ul Qur'an (Memorization)

Karatun haddar Alƙur'ani mai tsarki

Gabatarwa:

Darussan haddar Alƙur'ani (Hifz) a Maahadu sun haɗa da koyar da yadda ake haddace Alƙur'ani gaba ɗaya, tun daga farko har zuwa ƙarshe, tare da koyar da matakan Tajweed da yadda ake gyara karatun. Manufar mu ita ce, a koyar da ɗalibi ya haddace Alƙur'ani da tsari da kyau, ta yadda zai riƙe karatunsa har abada.

Wannan shiri yana dacewa da kowa, tun daga yara har zuwa manya, waɗanda suke da sha'awar haddace Littafin Allah. Muna da tsare-tsare na musamman da kuma malamai masu ƙwarewa waɗanda zasu taimaka wa ɗalibai a kowane mataki.

"Kuma hakika mun saukar da Alƙur'ani domin tunatarwa, shin akwai mai karɓar tunatarwa?"
— Surah Al-Qamar 54:17

"Mafi alherin ku shine wanda ya koyi Alƙur'ani sannan ya koyar da shi."
— Hadisin Annabi (SAW)

Tajweed & Qira'at (Recitation)

Karatun Tajweed da fassarar haruffa

Gabatarwa:

Darussan Tajweed a Maahadu suna koyar da yadda ake karanta Alƙur'ani daidai, kamar yadda Annabi (SAW) ya karanta. Wannan yana haɗa da sanin inda haruffa suke fitowa (makharij al-huruf), da kuma dokokin (ahkam) da suka shafi nun, mim, mudud, da sauran su. Yin karatun da Tajweed wajibi ne ga kowane Musulmi.

Muna bayar da dama ga ɗalibai su karanta wa malamai, su gyara kura-kuransu, kuma su koyi daban-daban Qira'at (nau'o'in karatu) kamar Hafs, Warsh, da sauransu.

"Kuma ka karanta Alƙur'ani da karatun hankali."
— Surah Al-Muzammil 73:4

"Duk wanda ya karanta littafin Allah, yana da lada guda goma a kowane harafi."
— Hadisin Tirmidhi

Tafsir & Ulum al-Qur'an (Qur'anic Studies)

Fassarar Alƙur'ani da iliman sa

Gabatarwa:

Darussan Tafsir suna taimaka wa ɗalibai su fahimci ma'anar Alƙur'ani da sakon da ke cikinsa. Wannan yana ba da haske kan dalilan saukar ayoyi, da kuma yadda za a yi amfani da su a rayuwar yau da kullum. Muna koyar da fassarar Alƙur'ani ta hanyar da ta dace da Fahimtar Magabata (Salafus-Salih).

Baya ga Tafsir, muna koyar da sauran ilimai kamar **Aqida** (fahimtar tauhidi), **Fiqh** (fikihu), da kuma **Tarbiyyah** (tarbiyya da da'a). Wannan yana taimaka wa ɗalibai su zama cikakkun Musulmai masu ilimi da kuma aiki.

"Littafi mai albarka ne da muka saukar maka don su yi tunani a kan ayoyinsa."
— Surah Sad 38:29

"Kada ku ce mutumin da ya haddace Alƙur'ani bai yi komai ba, amma ku ce mutumin da ya haddace Alƙur'ani ya yi aiki da shi."
— Hadisi
